# TaxTruth India - Feature Catalog

Welcome to the **TaxTruth Premium Suite**. Below is a summary of all the advanced fintech tools and features currently built into the platform:

---

## 1. Premium Dashboard (The Control Center)
*   **Tax Health Score**: A visual health indicator (out of 100) showing how optimized the user's current tax profile is.
*   **Actionable Missing Deductions Checklist**: A warning board that detects unutilized tax deductions (e.g., Parent's Health Insurance under Section 80D or Employer NPS under 80CCD(2)) and details the exact potential savings.
*   **Quick Launch Hub**: Easy access grid to load any of the advanced modules.

## 2. Deep Tax Calculator (Regime Comparator)
*   **Monthly Take-Home Intake**: Starts directly from what hits the user's bank account instead of asking for ambiguous CTC numbers.
*   **Step-by-Step Deduction Capture**: A wizard collecting HRA details, Section 80C, NPS, Health Insurance, Education Loans, and Perquisites.
*   **Side-by-Side Comparison**: Computes and contrasts tax liability under both the **Old Regime** and the **New Regime** down to the single rupee.

## 3. Form 16 "Magic Fill" (OCR Simulator)
*   **Drag & Drop Area**: A clean, responsive file-drop zone accepting Form 16 PDFs.
*   **Scanning Animation**: Simulated futuristic scanning sequences highlighting active Part A and Part B extraction.
*   **Automated Auto-Fill**: Parses data parameters to pre-fill the entire step-by-step calculator wizard, saving the user from manual typing.

## 4. Equity Capital Gains Tracker
*   **STCG (Short-Term Capital Gains)**: Computes flat 20% taxes on equity/mutual fund profits held for less than 1 year.
*   **LTCG (Long-Term Capital Gains)**: Calculates the new 12.5% tax rate on equity profits held for over 1 year, accounting for the new **₹1.25 Lakh tax-free exemption limit**.

## 5. CTC Restructuring Engine (CTC Optimizer)
*   **Salary Optimizer**: Accepts a user's total CTC and generates an optimized structure side-by-side with typical unoptimized setups.
*   **Tax-Free Shift**: Reallocates taxable "Special Allowance" into tax-free components like Employer NPS (10% of Basic), Meal Coupons (Sodexo/Zeta), and LTA.
*   **HR Email Template Generator**: Creates a copy-pasteable email template containing the proposed salary structure to send directly to HR.

## 6. Educational Tax Library (The Pocket Book)
*   **EL5 (Explain Like I'm 5) Explanations**: Removes financial jargon to make complex tax slabs accessible to beginners.
*   **CA "Golden Rules"**: Tips and hacks relating to Section 89 relief, Marginal relief, and structuring perquisites.
*   **Mobile-Responsive Navigation**: Interactive sidebar layout designed for desktop and mobile reading.

## 7. ITR Masterclass & CA Playbook (ITR Academy)
*   **Interactive Form Finder**: A simple 6-question profiler directing users to ITR-1, ITR-2, ITR-3, or ITR-4.
*   **Refund Maximizer Playbook**: CA-backed recommendations detailing how to override HRA, claim parents' senior bills, and set-off capital losses.
*   **10-Point Readiness Checklist**: An interactive compliance audit sheet covering AIS, Form 26AS matching, Form 10E, and foreign assets.
*   **Portal Filing Step-by-Step Guide**: Sequential walk-through explaining login, verification, and offline utility uploads on the official e-filing site.
